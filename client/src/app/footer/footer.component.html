UV Vote

The online voting system is made for the people of country residing around the world and want to vote for their representative. In this system the voter do not have to go to the polling booth to cast their vote. It also minimizes on errors of vote counting. The individual votes are submitted in a database which can be queried to find out who are the aspirants for a given post has the highest number of votes.
